Empowering Learners in Language, ICT, and Beyond!

Are you a francophone student studying in Ghana and looking to improve your English? Or are you a professional, student, or traveler eager to master French?

At SMAid Academy in Sunyani, we offer high-quality language training tailored to your needs—so you no longer have to travel to Accra to access top-tier courses!

While we are starting with English and French language programs, SMAid Academy is expanding to include ICT training, capacity building, and vocational skills development. Our goal is to equip learners with the tools they need for success in today’s world.

More Than an Academy – A Mission for Impact

SMAid Academy is a social enterprise under SMAid International, a non-profit organization committed to empowering communities through education, skills training, and development programs. Revenue generated from the Academy helps fund SMAid Int’l’s initiatives, creating lasting impact in underserved communities.

By choosing SMAid Academy, you’re not just investing in your future—you’re also supporting a greater cause!
